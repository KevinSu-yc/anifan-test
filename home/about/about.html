About AniFan

AniFan

AniFan is a website created by a big animation fans. This site mainly shares animation movies and TV series as recommendation for people who are interested but new to animations. This website includes pages that introduce plots, characters, and images of each animation.In addition, we also provide information about the studios that made the animations shared here. Furthermore, other animation fans are also welcome to visit and explore contents that might fit their interests.

We will do our best provide news about upcoming movies and TV series, meanwhile, update new recommendations every month. Hope you have fun exploring a little part of the animation world here!
